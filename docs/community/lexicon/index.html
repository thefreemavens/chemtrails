  • Maven

    One who is experienced or knowledgeable.2

    The word Maven comes from the Yiddish Meyvn, meaning “one who understands”. To be a maven you have to more than just understand a topic, you have to know its ins and outs. Mavens are often are the people that you turn to as experts in a field. You don’t become a maven overnight. That kind of expertise understanding comes with an accumulation of knowledge over the years.3
